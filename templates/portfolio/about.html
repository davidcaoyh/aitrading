"Portfolio Details" provides detailed and historical information about the assets in your account. There are two main panels on this page:

  1. Portfolio Performance: Tracks the historical performance of each asset in its local currency. Also tracks total portfolio value in CAD as well as the historical USD → CAD rate. To compare the performance of securities, simply click on a line item in the Compare to field on the left
  2. Portfolio Snapshot: View the value and details of your portfolio on any business day in the past year. Also shows a breakdown of the portfolio by security, currency, sector and asset category.
