"Portfolio Details" provides detailed and historical information about the assets in your account. There are three main panels on this page:

  1. Portfolio Performance: Tracks the historical performance of each asset in its local currency. Also tracks total portfolio value in CAD as well as the historical USD → CAD rate. To compare the performance of securities, simply click on a line item in the Compare to field on the left
  2. Portfolio Snapshot: View the value and details of your portfolio on any business day in the past year. Also shows a breakdown of the portfolio by security, currency, sector and asset category. Note that any pending trades items are grouped with the respective cash items in the charts
  3. Export Data: Allows you to export historical information about your portfolio in two formats.
    All Details will have one row per security per day representing all available information about that security.
    Time Series contains one row per date with columns describing the value of each security in the portfolio on that date. You can select from multiple different identifiers for the column headers that represent each security as well as numerous values to populate with.
